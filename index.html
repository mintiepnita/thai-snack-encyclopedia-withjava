GAIYANG


'Gai Yang' means 'grilled chicken' in Thai. This is a crispy snack with light smell of grilled chicken and a sweet taste of chilli dipping sauce which comes along in the same pack.


EURO CUSTARD CAKE


'Euro Custard Cake' is a sweet & Soft cream puff cake comes with individual pack of every piece makes it convenience to bring along. In each chew, you will find a sweet custard filled.


PU-THAI


'Pu-Thai' simply means "Thai crab" which is very obvious as its look, a little crab chip shape with a taste of squid scent and left you with a little sweet & salty in a bite.


JOLLYSTICK


'Jollystick' is a fruit flavored chewy lollipop. There are wrapped in colorful animal paper with 4 flavors: pineapple, orange, grape, strawberry.


LUCKY COCO


'Lucky Coco' is cocoa-flavored granulated sugar. There is only one flavor, cocoa. The taste of it is a combination of cocoa and chocolate. You might find it very sweet.


BANANA


'Banana' is a crispy banana chips. They come with 10 flavors: Original (Salty), Paprika, BBQ, Laab, Pizza, Seaweed, Cheese, Tom yum, Shrimp, and Chicken.


TEN JANG


'Ten Jang' is fish fillets come in 3 popular flavors: BBQ, cuttlefish, and chicken. "Ten Jang' fish fillets are packaged as grab-and-go everywhere.


RINGPOP


'Ringpop' is a lollipop shaped as a ring. They come with 4 flavors: Strawberry, Grape, Fabric, Cola. The candy has a mild fruity aroma and a sweet and sour taste.


YIMYIM


'Yimyim' is a biscuit stick dipped in cocoa cream flavor. When eating, take a biscuit stick from one side of the package to dip it in with cocoa cream on another side of the package.


KIDKID


'Kidkid' is a peppermint flavoured gum. With their iconic slogan, 'KidKid your chew friend. If you want to chew, chew KidKid', KidKid became one of the popular gum of Thai kids.


SANGHAI


'Sanghai' is a chocolate cream wafers in an individual package to be grab-and-go everywhere you need it. It is flavored with chocolatey, creamy and lightly sweet tastes.


FF TOMATO CRACKERS


'FF Tomato Crackers' is a simple chip comes with a special tomato taste with no spicy but sweet, salty, and a little sour which gives you a hint of the flavor.
